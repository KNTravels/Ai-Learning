Unsupervised Learning

Machine Learning 🌱 Beginner to Advanced Last Updated: 2024

Overview

Less than 5 Years Experience

Unsupervised learning finds patterns in unlabeled data. No correct answers provided. Includes clustering (grouping similar items) and dimensionality reduction.

5-15 Years Experience

Algorithms: K-means, hierarchical clustering, DBSCAN, PCA, t-SNE. Used for data exploration, anomaly detection, and recommendation systems.

15+ Years Experience

Advanced techniques: mixture models, manifold learning, and self-supervised learning. Implements variational inference and probabilistic graphical models.

Quick Facts

  • Category: Machine Learning
  • Key Tags: Clustering, Dimensionality Reduction, Pattern Discovery
  • Experience Levels: All Levels
  • Real-world Use: Widely used across industries
